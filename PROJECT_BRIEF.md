# Life Journey Dashboard

This is a private personal web dashboard made only for me.

The goal is to help me:
- Track my life journey
- Understand myself better
- Improve myself
- Reflect on my past
- Stay focused in the present
- Build my future self
- Stay disciplined with gym, habits, goals, learning, and health

The app should feel like:
- Personal Life OS
- Self-improvement dashboard
- Digital journal
- Habit tracker
- Goal tracker
- Life timeline
- Gym progress tracker
- Private autobiography
- Personal command center

Core questions:
1. Where am I in life right now?
2. What am I trying to improve?
3. Am I becoming better or repeating old patterns?
4. What have I learned from my past?
5. What kind of person am I becoming?

Design direction:
Use design.md exactly.

The app should feel:
- Private
- Mature
- Calm
- Personal
- Focused
- Editor-like
- Emotionally meaningful
- Useful every day

It should not feel:
- Generic
- Colorful
- Cheesy
- Like a normal SaaS dashboard
- Like a social media app
